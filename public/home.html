Our Goal

A community of genuine individuals reaching out to provide or receive assistance across all walks of life.

Our Values

Due to the potentially sensitive nature of the topics discussed in our community, we strive to ensure that responses are all constructive. We heavily moderate against trolls, fake accounts, and abusive users.

The Flow

Using our site is really simple; just log in, click whether or not you want help or would like to recieve help and you're on your way.

No Hassle Signup

Our sign up process is as simple as having a Facebook account, and logging in through that. No additional info required.
